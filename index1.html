Features

Augmented Reality is meant to enhance what we already see. This is either through a device or a screen, usually your mobile phone. We ask a simple question to ourselves: How can we enhance something that cannot be touched/felt, but only visualized through a medium?

No App download required

All our AR experiences are on web, so there is no need to download a separate App

QR code based

As QR code has become most used medium, we utilize its power to connect our users with AR experiences

Customized AR Experiences

We provide customized AR products where users can customize what they see in AR

No registration required

We don't want you to create any account to enjoy AR experiences on our platform
